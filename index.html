3-minute promotion story

The case for Managing Director rests on four things: the breadth of what Pierre leads, the depth of the relationships he holds, his courage to pursue what others haven't attempted, and the calibre of people he builds around him.

In FY26, Pierre's accountability spans further than any single programme. He leads a 50-person Sanofi team delivering OneLIMS platform work and — uniquely — AI agents in production within the Sanofi environment, the only Accenture team on the account to achieve this. He holds oversight on a 15-person Pfizer programme, mentors two senior managers in the Gallia region, co-leads the Labs Ireland community of ~20 Irish resources, and serves as Global Lead for Integrated Talent Management across the entire Labs Reinvention practice. Across all dimensions, Pierre's direct and indirect accountability extends to over 100 people — across multiple clients, geographies and practice responsibilities simultaneously.

Commercially, Pierre has closed over $7M across Sanofi and Pfizer — with a further $3.18M in active H2 pipeline, putting total tracked revenue at $10.2M against a $12M target. Every opportunity was originated by Pierre. A standout example is the Sanofi acceleration initiative: Pierre designed and facilitated the workshops, prepared the business case, presented to senior leadership, and convinced them of the vision. That initiative is now approved, deployed, and actively winning new business.

The people data is equally compelling. Pierre's Labs team has performed above the 50th Accenture percentile for two consecutive fiscal cycles. The OneLIMS 2024 project sits at the 82nd percentile with an eNPS of 64. His Action Seen score of 4.29 is above both the Sanofi account and Accenture averages. As Global ITM Lead, he has standardised onboarding across the Labs Reinvention practice — a role he volunteered for.

Sami Hamani, Global Client Account Lead for Sanofi, has backed Pierre as a Priority 1 candidate for two consecutive promotion cycles, pointing specifically to his ability to build strong, deep working relationships with key senior stakeholders. The recommendation is clear: Pierre is operating at Managing Director level today. This promotion is recognition of reality.
